About Us

Buster was a beloved German Shepherd pup from my family—a relative's dog who became much more than that. Through him, I saw firsthand the true spirit of dogs: loving, loyal, and full of life. Buster taught me that dogs are emotional beings who deserve the same kindness, respect, and quality care we give ourselves, especially when it comes to their diet. His life inspired a commitment to create Bark with Buster—a brand dedicated to making natural, wholesome treats that honour his memory and help dogs everywhere live healthier, happier lives.
